Privacy Policy for StockFinderAI

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

1. Introduction

StockFinderAI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

StockFinderAI allows you to create a personal watchlist of stock symbols. This data includes:

  • Stock symbols you add to your watchlist
  • Company names associated with saved stocks

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect any personal information, usage analytics, or tracking data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The watchlist data you create is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Never shared with anyone
  • Deleted permanently when you uninstall the app

4. Data Storage

All user preferences and watchlist data are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We do not maintain any servers or databases containing your personal information.

5. Third-Party Data Sources

StockFinderAI displays financial information from the following publicly available sources:

  • Yahoo Finance: Stock prices, market data, and historical charts
  • Congressional Trading Data: Publicly disclosed congressional stock trades
  • OpenInsider: Publicly disclosed corporate insider trading information

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We do not share any user data because we do not collect any personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

StockFinderAI is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can view all your saved watchlist items in the app
  • Deletion: You can remove individual stocks or uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Portability: Your data remains on your device and is not locked to our service

9. Security

Your watchlist data is stored securely on your device using industry-standard local storage mechanisms provided by your device's operating system (iOS or Android).

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Disclaimer

StockFinderAI provides financial data and analysis for general informational purposes only. It does not provide investment, financial, legal, or tax advice. The app displays publicly available information and should not be considered as personalized investment advice.
